Best chefs

Gordon Ramsey

A Scottish-born British chef, restaurateur, television presenter, and author. Known for his fiery and demanding personality as well as his exquisite cuisine, Ramsay has been awarded 17 Michelin stars, the highest culinary accolade, and currently heads a restaurant empire around the world.

Jamie Oliver

British chef known for his infectious enthusiasm, approachable cooking style, and dedication to healthy eating. He's a champion for fresh ingredients and simple techniques, often showcasing his passion through cookbooks, restaurants, and ongoing campaigns to improve school lunches.

Ferran Adrià

A highly influential Spanish chef, rose to fame for his innovative and often deconstructivist approach to cuisine. Born in Barcelona in 1962, Adrià revolutionized the culinary world by pushing boundaries with techniques, textures, and unexpected flavor combinations.

Ming Tsai

Ming Tsai is a Chinese-American celebrity chef known for his innovative fusion cuisine that blends East Asian flavors with Western techniques. Born in Taiwan and raised in Connecticut, Tsai's culinary journey reflects this cultural bridge.

Joan Roca

Spanish chef renowned for his artistry and meticulous approach to cuisine. Joan's cooking is known for its focus on fresh, seasonal ingredients, meticulous plating, and a deep exploration of flavor and texture. He is a champion for sustainability and ethical sourcing while staying true to his Catalan heritage.

Mashama Bailey

Mashama Bailey is an American chef trained in French technique who is currently cooking Southern cuisine. In 2019, Bailey was awarded a James Beard Award as best chef of the southeast. In 2022, Bailey was awarded a James Beard Award as Outstanding Chef.
